Help & Guide

Welcome to Dreamtech Freelance Solutions! This page will guide you through the basic features and usage.

1. Getting Started

  • Download and open the app.
  • Sign up with your email or log in if you already have an account.
  • Set up your profile (photo, name, preferences).

2. Using the App

  • Dashboard: Get an overview of your activity and updates.
  • Create Posts: Click the "+" button to post images, text, or videos.
  • View Status: Tap any user profile to view their status updates.

3. Profile & Settings

  • Edit your profile by tapping the profile icon.
  • Customize app settings like notifications, privacy, and appearance.
  • Change your password anytime under Account Settings.

4. Notifications

You'll receive alerts for:

  • New messages
  • Likes and comments
  • Status views and follows

5. Need Help?

If you have any issues or suggestions, contact us at:

Email: support@myapp.com

FAQ: Available under the Help section in the app menu.
